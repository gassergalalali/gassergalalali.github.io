About Me

I am a Visiting Assistant Professor in the Civil Engineering Department at the University of Texas Rio Grande Valley (UTRGV).

My background is rooted in the construction engineering and management domain and expands into broad multi-disciplinary research. I am particularly interested in creating complex system-of-systems simulations using agent-based modeling to solve energy-related multidisciplinary problems, among other topics, and I am eager to expand my skills and knowledge into new emerging research topics.

I received my Ph.D. in Civil Engineering from Missouri University of Sciene and Technology. My advisor is Dr. Islam El-adaway. Before pursuing a PhD, I received my M.Sc. in Construction Engineering from The American University in Cairo under the supervision of Dr. Samer Ezeldin.

My Research Interests

  • Construction Engineering and Management
  • Computer Modeling and Simulation
  • Distributed Solar Generation and Wholesale Power Markets
  • Machine Learning and AI
  • Contractual and Legal Affairs
