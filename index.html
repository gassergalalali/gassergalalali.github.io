About Me

I am currently doing my PhD in Civil Engineering, specializing in Construction Engineering and Management, at Missouri University of Sciene and Technology. My advisor is Dr. Islam El-adaway. Before pursuing a PhD, I received my M.Sc. in Construction Engineering from The American University in Cairo under the supervision of Dr. Samer Ezeldin.

My research interests include:

  • Computer Modeling and Simulation
  • Computational Intelligence
  • Machine Learning
  • Optimization
  • Contractual and Legal Affairs
